Help & FAQ

  • How do I level up?
    Post, comment, complete tasks, and participate in challenges to earn XP and coins.
  • How do I earn coins?
    Daily login, posting, completing tasks, and claiming gifts all earn coins.
  • What are roles?
    "Sissy" and "Master" are the main roles. Masters can invest in sissies; sissies can be invested in and complete tasks.
  • How does the market work?
    Masters invest coins in sissies. Sissies gain value as they level up and participate.
  • How do I report abuse?
    Use the report button on posts or threads, or contact an admin via the forum.

Still need help? Contact support@goodgurl.gg.
